About Us

TK360 is a local residential and commercial remodeling company founded in September 2014.

Since its founding, TK360 has successfully completed kitchen updates, bathroom remodels, basement renovations, roof replacement, new decks and patios, home expansions, floor-plan redesigns, and a variety of other major home renovations.

We are proud to put the same time and attention to minor projects such as drywall repair, painting, appliance installation, tiling, and more. No project is too small or too big, we treat all projects the same to achieve top quality results that our clients envision. TK360 where the only corners we cut are the ones that make your project look sharp!

TK360 employs licensed contractors and subcontractors with specialized expertise who all strive to complete our clients’ projects with quality, on time, and within budget.

TK360 LLC is the premier home remodeling company serving Washington DC and the surrounding area. When you need a minor update done to your old kitchen cabinets or a complete makeover of your bathroom, just know that we’re the contractors that can get the job done right the first time for you!

We’re a full-service company that specializes in interior and exterior home improvements. The primary services to redesign your property include bathroom remodeling, kitchen remodeling, flooring, drywall installation, painting, cabinet refinishing, and more.

Additionally, we offer a few handyman services such as house repair, home glass repair, minor electrical repair, flooring, and plumbing. Our mission is to make your dream home renovation project become a reality.

TK360’s key values are integrity, reliability, and a strong customer relationship. These three values are the foundation of our company and are a daily reminder of who we are and what we are about. We strongly believe that our company is successful because of the relationships that we develop with our clients.

While providing superior construction services in and around VA, with loyal customer service, TK360’s mission is to deliver cost-effective solutions to your every remodeling need. By building your vision into a reality, we guarantee the highest satisfaction with your finished project.
